Soundest is free and easy-to-use email marketing service for small and medium ecommerce sites. 

== Description ==

**Unique in-store email builder: just browse your store and add items to your newsletter with a click of a button**
**Sales tracking and customer segmentation: all automated for your convenience**
**Free of charge: send 100K emails per month at no cost**


Make creation of email newsletters easier and less time-consuming with Soundest app for Woocommerce stores. Start using it in seconds. No need to code, design or build anything – Soundest app works out of the box!

**Unique in-store email builder**

Just browse your store and select items that you want to add to your email marketing campaign. We'll pick them up and put in your newsletter complete with pictures, descriptions and pricing. This will save up to 90% of your time compared to other email template editors.

**Prebuilt customizable templates**

Create good-looking newsletters by choosing among our prebuilt templates. Customize them to match your shop style to look even better. Your newsletter will be ready to be sent in minutes.

**Complete email marketing toolbox**

Find all of your favorite easy-to-use email marketing features inside. Email scheduling, out-of-the box customer segmentation, sales tracking and detailed reports – everything is here.

**Guaranteed deliverability**

Be sure that newsletters reach your customers. We're strict on spam in order to provide you with benefits of top class email deliverability and bounce management.



== Installation ==

1. Upload `soundest.zip` to the Wordpress
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Launch it by selecting Woocommerce > Soundest from your Wordpress menu.
